## Slide 3 — The product

- One brief in. One live URL out. Average turnaround: 27 min.
- Output is on the buyer's domain, in a real GitHub repo, with real analytics and real TLS.
- Brand identity is generated **first** — palette, typography, voice — and then the page is laid out around it.
- The buyer never sees a template gallery.
